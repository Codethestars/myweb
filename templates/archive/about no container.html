About Engineer's Toolbox

Engineer's Toolbox is a collaborative platform created by practicing professionals in engineering, architecture, and building systems design. We understand the challenges of modern MEP projects and are committed to providing practical tools that streamline your workflow.

Our Mission

To empower building systems professionals with intuitive tools that enhance productivity and support informed decision-making in MEP design processes. We believe that by simplifying routine calculations and providing quick reference guides, we can help you focus on what truly matters - creating innovative and efficient building solutions.

Built by Professionals, for Professionals

Our platform brings together the collective expertise of mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, plumbing specialists, architects, and construction professionals. Every tool and reference guide is crafted based on real-world experience and practical applications in the field.
